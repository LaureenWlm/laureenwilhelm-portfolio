About

Capturing the unseen
side of women's sport.

I'm Laureen, a French freelance sports photographer specialised in women's football. My work blends action, portraiture and reportage into a single cinematic narrative — creative, intentional, and emotionally true.

Official photographer for SKN St. Pölten and the ADMIRAL Frauen Bundesliga during the 2025/2026 season, I have also documented UWCL matches across Europe. I also co-founded ORA, an image agency dedicated exclusively to women's sport.
